You should be able to wear an empty bag over your head to blind yourself, but
this sets charisma to 6. If you wear an oilskin sack on your head, it starts a
strangulation timer. If you wear a bag of tricks on your head, you get YAFM and
instadeath, or possibly it just constantly bites your head.
