Split up the effects of magic resistance, as it is too extensive. It protects
against magical force, death magic, teleportation, polymorph, etc.
* Death resistance should be conferred by a helm, an amulet, maaaaaybe as an
  intrinsic gained only through crowning.
* Force or magic missile resistance: The things that should provide this are
  unspecified.
* Teleportation resistance: The things that should provide this are unspecified.
* Polymorph resistance: The things that should provide this are unspecified.
