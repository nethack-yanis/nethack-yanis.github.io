Provide some ways for Vecna to come back after being defeated:
1. Allow arch-liches to grow up into Vecna if Vecna does not currently exist.
2. Any humanoid caster that acquires the Hand or Eye of Vecna should instantly
   become Vecna. Covetous liches should also covet those artifacts.
