Beholder corpses have a chance of conveying a Charisma increase accompanied, if
you are hallucinating, by the message "Beauty is truly in the eye of the
beholder." Alternatively, do not have a message, but add this line as a comment
in the source explaining why a beholder would grant Charisma.
