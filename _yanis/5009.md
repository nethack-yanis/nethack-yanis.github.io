Scroll of fusion, which allow you to combine charged items of the same type
together, summing their charges (for instance, two 0:7 wands of digging will
fuse into a 0:14 wand of digging). They can only generate in Gehennom to keep
them a decidedly late-game object. Not specified if they should bypass ordinary
rules around items that explode when overcharged.
