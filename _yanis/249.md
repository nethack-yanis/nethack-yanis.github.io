Bed, a <span class="nhsym clr-red">\\</span> which if you sit on it makes you
fall asleep for d10 turns or you have fully recovered your health. While
sleeping on the bed, you get the same effects as hungerless regeneration. Found
in barracks and some special levels. Breakfast not provided.
