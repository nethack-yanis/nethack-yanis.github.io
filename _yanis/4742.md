If a monster is decapitated by Vorpal Blade and you are not deaf but can't see
the monster for whatever reason (whether or not you were the one wielding it, or
it happened out of your sight elsewhere on the level), you should get the
message "You hear a snicker-snack!" or just "Snicker-snack!"
