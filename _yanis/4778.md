Since cartomancers can get mail daemons as pets from randomly dropped summon
cards, but the mail daemon is a useless pet because it has no attacks, there
should be a new monster either as a grown-up form or replacing mail daemons
entirely, called a "postmaster", which is a useful and capable pet.
