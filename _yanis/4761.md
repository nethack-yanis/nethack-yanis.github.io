Higher monster difficulty sacrifices should have higher odds that a sacrifice
will be given than lower-difficulty sacrifices.

(Note that this is not the same as the NetHack 3.7.0 feature in which
higher-difficulty sacrifices have a larger, better pool of artifacts that can be
given.)
